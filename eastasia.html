East Asia

East Asia is home to a wide array of cultures, each of them connected in many ways. These include China, Japan, Taiwan, Mongolia, and Korea.

Japan

Explore foods like Sushi, Tempura, and Ramen.

Korea

Discover regional favorites like Kimchi, Bulgogi, and Bibimbap.

China

Enjoy Dumplings, Peking Duck, and Mapo Tofu, along with a rich variety of other local dishes.

Mongolia

Savor Khorkhog, Boortsog, and Buuz.

Taiwan

Explore foods like Beef Noodle Soup, Stinky Tofu, and Pineapple Cake.
