Food from Africa

Africa is home to a wide array of cultures, per square mile, more than any other continent on Earth. This is only a small sample of foods from that area.

Togo

Explore foods like Akkra, Fufu, and Koklo Meme.

South Africa

Discover regional favorites like Bunny Chow, Bobotie, and Biltong.

Mali

Enjoy Jollof Rice, Yassa, and Maafe, along with a rich variety of local dishes.

The Congo

Savor Moambe Chicken, Fufu, and Pondu.

Nigeria

Explore foods like Egusi Soup, Suya, and Pounded Yam.
