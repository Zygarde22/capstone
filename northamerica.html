Food from North America

North America, which includes Canada, the United States, Mexico, and the Caribbean islands, is home to a wide array of foods.

Canada

Explore foods like poutine, butter tarts, and maple syrup delicacies.

United States

Discover regional favorites like barbecue, Tex-Mex, and clam chowder.

Mexico

Enjoy tacos, mole, tamales, and a rich variety of spicy dishes.

Caribbean Islands

Savor jerk chicken, plantains, and tropical fruit dishes.
